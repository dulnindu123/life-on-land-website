💧 Water Cycling & Purification

Water is constantly on the move, cycling through evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and infiltration. Nature's water cycle ensures clean, fresh water flows through forests, rivers, soil, and underground reservoirs — keeping all life hydrated and healthy.

🌦️ Nature's Process

  • Evaporation: Sunlight turns water into vapor
  • Condensation: Vapor forms clouds
  • Precipitation: Rain replenishes Earth
  • Filtration: Soil and plants purify water naturally

💦 Nature's Water Purification Machine

Wetlands, forests, and even soil act like living filters. They remove impurities, balance pH, and recharge groundwater. Without these natural systems, clean drinking water would be harder — and more expensive — to get.

🚿 How You Can Help

  • Fix leaks and use water-efficient appliances to conserve fresh water.
  • Avoid dumping chemicals or waste into drains, lakes, or rivers to prevent pollution.
  • Support reforestation and wetland restoration to enhance natural filtration systems.
  • Educate your community about the importance of protecting our water cycle.

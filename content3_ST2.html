🌾 Soil Health & Food Security

Soil isn’t just dirt — it's a living system that holds the key to human survival. Healthy soil stores water, supports plant growth, cycles nutrients, and even captures carbon. When soil thrives, so does life.

🌱 Why Healthy Soil Matters

  • Enables crops to grow with better nutrients
  • Improves water retention and reduces erosion
  • Supports biodiversity below and above ground
  • Reduces reliance on chemical fertilizers

🍽️ Soil & Food Security

Over 95% of our food comes from the soil. But with land degradation, urbanization, and poor farming practices, the soil is losing its strength — putting global food supplies at risk. By restoring soil health, we ensure access to affordable, nutritious food for all.

🌍 What Can You Do?

  • Support local farms using sustainable practices
